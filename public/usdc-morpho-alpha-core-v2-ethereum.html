About USDC Alpha Core V2

USDC Alpha Core V2 is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Morpho strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
