About ETH Lend

ETH Lend is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Arcadia strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $175K in deposits and is generating 2.66% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 3.76%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 3.86% since inception.
