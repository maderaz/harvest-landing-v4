About ETH Aerodrome

ETH Aerodrome is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Aerodrome strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $1K in deposits and is generating 18.46% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 15.97%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 199.52% since inception.
