Ranking Rules

Editorial filters applied to every public ranking surface (homepage, asset hubs, network hubs, related vaults, ticker strip, sitemap). Rules are configuration-as-code: flipping a switch requires a commit and a rebuild.

Live only

A vault must have a positive 24h APY and a positive TVL to appear in any ranking. Catches dead deployments, brand-new vaults with no observations, and API rows where data is missing.

src/lib/data.ts → isLiveVault

On

Stale APY (14+ days unchanged)

If a vault's daily APY value hasn't moved for 14+ consecutive days the strategy is almost certainly paused, dead, or oracle-stuck. Filtered out of every list and re-included automatically the moment the APY moves.

src/lib/data.ts → isStaleApyHistory

On

Broken low-TVL (sub $10k, flat history)

Any vault with under $10,000 TVL whose APY history shows 14+ identical daily observations in the last 30 days is treated as broken (paused harvest, oracle stuck, or never bootstrapped). Filtered from rankings, dropped from the sitemap, and emitted with robots noindex on the product page so crawlers stop wasting budget.

src/lib/data.ts → isBrokenLowTvlVault

On

Hide Aerodrome strategies

Operator decision: Aerodrome-routed strategies are excluded from public rankings. Match is a case-insensitive substring on productName/category, so any future Aerodrome listing is hidden automatically until this rule is flipped off.

src/lib/data.ts → isAerodromeVault

On
Currently matching 22 of 163 indexed vaults. All hidden from public surfaces.

How to flip a rule

Edit the constants at the top of src/lib/admin-rules.ts, commit, push to main. Vercel rebuilds and the new ruleset is live within a minute or two. The next hourly cron will pick up the change automatically.
