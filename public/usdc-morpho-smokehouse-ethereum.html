Frequently Asked Questions

What's the current APY?

USDC Smokehouse is showing a 24-hour APY of 4.42% with a 30-day average of 5.55%. Rates are variable and move with market conditions, liquidity, and the underlying protocol's incentives.

How does this vault work?

What are the strategy risks?

No DeFi yield strategy is risk-free. The main vectors that apply to USDC Smokehouse are smart-contract risk (a bug or upgrade in the vault or the underlying Morpho protocol could cause loss of funds), oracle risk (mis-priced price feeds can drain a strategy in a single block), liquidity risk (large withdrawals at the wrong moment can clip the share-price), depeg risk (the underlying USDC de-pegging from its target value), and governance risk (admin keys, parameter changes, fee switches). Risk levels we display on the site are editorial classifications, not a quantitative model.
