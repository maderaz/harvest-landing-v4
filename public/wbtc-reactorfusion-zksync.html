About WBTC ReactorFusion

WBTC ReactorFusion is a autocompounder vault on zkSync that accepts BTC deposits and routes them into the ReactorFusion strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into BTC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
