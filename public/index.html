Frequently Asked Questions

About yield, risk, and our methodology
What is Harvest Finance?

Harvest is an onchain yield index that currently tracks 125 DeFi yield strategies across 6 networks, representing $10.0M in tracked TVL. The strategies in the index are spread across 18 distinct underlying protocols. Harvest has been operating onchain since 2020, 6+ years of continuous on-chain activity.

What is the best DeFi yield right now?

The highest 24-hour APY in our index right now is 23.80%, currently held by ETH Yearn OG V2 on Morpho. The average 24-hour APY across all 125 strategies we follow sits at 3.80%. The ranking changes as on-chain conditions shift; the table on this page is sorted by 24-hour APY by default and updates with every daily build.

How is APY calculated?

24-hour APY is the mean of APY records observed in the last 24 hours from our hosted indexer. 30-day APY is the simple arithmetic mean of daily APY observations over the last 30 days, filtering out negative values. APY is not time-weighted and does not account for compounding within the window. Reward tokens contribute at the rates published by the underlying protocol; we do not perform our own USD conversion of reward streams. The longest continuously-tracked strategy in our current index is ETH OnlyBoost at 1195+ days of APY history. Full methodology details are on the methodology page.

How often is the data updated?

Strategy data is refetched hourly from the underlying APIs and the site is rebuilt as a static export, so the version a visitor sees can lag the latest fetch by up to one hour. The current build reflects 125 live strategies across 6 networks. Real-time on-page values are not currently provided; the freshness line in the footer reflects this.

Are these yields safe?

No DeFi yield strategy is risk-free. Smart-contract risk, oracle risk, liquidity risk, depeg risk and governance risk all apply, and risk profiles vary between protocols and between deployments of the same protocol on different networks. Across the 125 strategies in our index today, all are operated by Harvest Finance, which we disclose openly. Per-strategy risk levels shown on the site are editorial classifications and are not yet derived from a quantitative model; the risk framework page covers the categories in more detail.

What chains and assets does Harvest cover?

We currently index strategies on 6 networks, with the largest coverage on Base, Ethereum and Arbitrum. The asset breakdown across the strategies we follow is USDC (68), ETH (35), USDT (12), BTC (10). ETH coverage includes WETH and major staking derivatives; BTC coverage includes WBTC, cbBTC and tBTC. Coverage expands as new strategies are added to the index.

Is Harvest a DeFi protocol or an aggregator?

Harvest is both. Every one of the 125 strategies listed on this site today is operated by Harvest Finance, which we disclose openly on the methodology page. Listing and ranking are not influenced by operator status because, at present, all listed strategies share the same operator. Future expansion to third-party operators will preserve neutral ranking.
