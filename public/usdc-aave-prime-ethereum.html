Frequently Asked Questions

What's the current APY?

USDC Prime is showing a 24-hour APY of 2.93% with a 30-day average of 4.74%. Rates are variable and move with market conditions, liquidity, and the underlying protocol's incentives.

It's a autocompounder on Ethereum operated by Harvest Finance. You deposit USDC, and the vault routes capital through the Aave strategy on your behalf - harvesting rewards, swapping them back to USDC, and redepositing without manual steps.

What are the strategy risks?

No DeFi yield strategy is risk-free. The main vectors that apply to USDC Prime are smart-contract risk (a bug or upgrade in the vault or the underlying Aave protocol could cause loss of funds), oracle risk (mis-priced price feeds can drain a strategy in a single block), liquidity risk (large withdrawals at the wrong moment can clip the share-price), depeg risk (the underlying USDC de-pegging from its target value), and governance risk (admin keys, parameter changes, fee switches). Risk levels we display on the site are editorial classifications, not a quantitative model.
