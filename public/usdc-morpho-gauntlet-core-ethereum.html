About USDC Gauntlet Core

USDC Gauntlet Core is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Morpho strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $151 in deposits and is generating 4.06% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 4.14%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 9.01% since inception.
