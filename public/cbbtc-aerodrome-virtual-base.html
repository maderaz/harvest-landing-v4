About cbBTC Aerodrome

cbBTC Aerodrome is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts BTC deposits and routes them into the Aerodrome strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into BTC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
