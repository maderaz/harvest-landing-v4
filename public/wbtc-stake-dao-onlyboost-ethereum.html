About WBTC OnlyBoost

WBTC OnlyBoost is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts BTC deposits and routes them into the Stake DAO strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into BTC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
