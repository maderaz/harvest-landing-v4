About USDT Steakhouse

USDT Steakhouse is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts USDT deposits and routes them into the Morpho strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDT and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $181K in deposits and is generating 3.48% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 4.36%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 20.03% since inception.
