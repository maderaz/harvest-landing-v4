About zkSync yield in this index

zkSync Era is a zk-rollup that settles to Ethereum. The strategies tracked here use zkSync-native lending and AMM venues; bridging in and out of zkSync uses validity proofs rather than the long withdrawal windows seen on optimistic rollups.

We currently track 8 yield strategies on zkSync, spanning USDC, ETH, USDT and BTC across 4 distinct protocols. The highest live APY in this set comes from USDT Aave at 3.29%; the average across the cohort is 1.21%.

Every strategy in the index is operated by Harvest Finance today; the zkSynccohort therefore reflects Harvest's zkSync deployments, not a survey of every yield source on the network. Inclusion criteria, APY and TVL handling, and ranking logic are documented on the methodology page.

Strategies in our zkSync cohort accept deposits in USDC, ETH, USDT and BTC. Each product page lists the underlying asset and any reward tokens emitted by the strategy.

Is yield farming on zkSync safe?

No DeFi yield strategy is risk-free. Smart-contract risk, oracle risk, liquidity risk, depeg risk, and governance risk all apply, and the same protocol can have different risk profiles on different networks. Per-strategy risk levels shown on this site are editorial classifications today; see the risk framework section of the methodology page for details.

How does zkSync compare to other networks for DeFi yield?

zkSync is one of several networks we cover. Comparisons across networks are most meaningful when controlled for asset (for example USDC on zkSync versus USDC on Ethereum) because asset-specific markets dominate the rate. The "Yield on other networks" links below this page are the easiest way to make those comparisons.

How is APY on zkSync calculated?

24-hour APY is the mean of APY records observed in the last 24 hours from our hosted indexer. 30-day APY is the simple arithmetic mean of daily APY observations over the last 30 days, filtering out negative values. Reward tokens contribute at the rates published by the underlying protocol; we do not perform our own USD conversion of reward streams. Full details are on the methodology page.
