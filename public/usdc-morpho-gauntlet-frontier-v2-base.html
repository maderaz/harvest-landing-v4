About USDC Gauntlet Frontier V2

USDC Gauntlet Frontier V2 is a autocompounder vault on Base that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Morpho strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $174K in deposits and is generating 16.62% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 15.29%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 274.12% since inception.
