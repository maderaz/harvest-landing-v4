About USDT Quickswap

USDT Quickswap is a autocompounder vault on Polygon that accepts USDT deposits and routes them into the Quickswap strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDT and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.
