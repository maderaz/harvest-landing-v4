About USDC Quickswap

USDC Quickswap is a autocompounder vault on Polygon that accepts USDC deposits and routes them into the Quickswap strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into USDC and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $3K in deposits and is generating 3.48% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 15.62%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 162.31% since inception.
