About ETH OnlyBoost

ETH OnlyBoost is a autocompounder vault on Ethereum that accepts ETH deposits and routes them into the Stake DAO strategy. The vault automatically harvests rewards, swaps them back into ETH and redeposits, compounding returns over time without manual harvesting or restaking.

The vault currently holds $89K in deposits and is generating 70.85% APY over the last 24 hours. The 30-day average APY sits at 20.11%, and over its lifetime share price has grown 45.51% since inception.
